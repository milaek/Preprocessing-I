Bio

Kate Matheson was born in London, England in 1996. Soon after, her family moved back to the United States of America, where her musical and theatrical journey would begin.

After several years building her skills with the Cantabile Youth Singers, Voice Teachers such as Patricia Urbano and Annelle Delfs, and youth theater companies such as the Peninsula Youth Theater and the Lucie Stern Children’s Theater,Kate went to study at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music under Patricia Craig. There, she earned her Bachelors of Music with a focus in Vocal Performance in 2018.

Since graduating, Kate has shifted her focus to a career Musical Theater. Most recently, she has performed as Phoebe D’ysquith in South Bay Musical Theater’s 2019 production of a Gentleman’s Guide to Love and Murder.
